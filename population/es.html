Evolution strategies algorithm

Intuition

The picture indicates x and y axis as well as the maximum of a fitness landscape (the rasigrin function). By clicking the button, you can see how Evolution Strategies optimizes a population of solutions until it reaches the maximum on the top left corner.


Details

In this process, Evolution Strategies start with a random parameter for the mean (red dot). The mean is then iteratively updated in the direction of solutions with higher quality (the direction of the maximum). The red dot indicates the current best solution.

There are several slightly different types of Evolution strategy algorithms. The type used in the animation is th OpenAI Evolution Strategy.
