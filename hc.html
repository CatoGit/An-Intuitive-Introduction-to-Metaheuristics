Hill-climbing algorithm

Intuition

Before you go into the details of the algorithm, try to get the ball to the top of the hill. Click on the ball to start the climb!

The quality of the solution is indicated by the red colour of the ball. The brighter the red, the higher the quality of the solution.

Hint: You need four clicks to get to the local maximum.

Details

"Hill-Climbing [...] is related to gradient ascent, but it doesn’t require you to know the strength of the gradient or even its direction: you just iteratively test new candidate solutions in the region of your current candidate, and adopt the new ones if they’re better. This enables you to climb up the hill until you reach a local optimum." (Luke, 2013)
